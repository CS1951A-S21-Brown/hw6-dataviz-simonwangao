1. I chose the Video Game Sales dataset.
  • For the first question, I gave user (which is my boss) the option to input a specific year (or all years together) to check the top 10 video games. Names, platforms and sales are clearly showed via lollipop charts. Irregular input will be rejected. Circles will be dimmed if user hovers her mouse prompt on it.
  • For the second question, I used a pie chart with sales being sorted. User can choose a time span and a region, and the pie chart will show the sorted popular genres with gradually changing color. Hovering mouse on it will show the tooltips, which show the genre names, global sales and their proportion percentage.
  • For the third question, use can choose a specific genre to see the top 10 publishers, with the best one being red noted. The barplot will show how much the best publisher outperforms the others.
